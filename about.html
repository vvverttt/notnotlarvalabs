Hi, we're Vert and Arkan!

DystoLabz is the home for our professional and experimental projects.

We're creative technologists that have worked on almost every kind of software. Examples include large scale web infrastructure, generative art, and blockchain collectibles. Our projects include Missing Phunks and DystoPhunks, crafted as Ethscription digital artifacts. We've also contributed to open source, participated in hackathons, and built unique digital experiences.

As a result of our work we've spoken at a wide range of venues and collaborated with amazing people in the crypto and tech space.

We've also been lucky enough to have long lasting client relationships with some of the best companies in the world. We're very proud of the work we've done with them, some of which may even be on your phone right now.
